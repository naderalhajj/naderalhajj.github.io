ABOUT RIVA

Al Othaim Holding
Since 1956

Al Othaim Holding Company has grown from a small local company to a billion-dollar conglomerate in the Saudi market. Its operations have expanded to include commercial, real estate, industrial, and investment activities. The company's publicly traded subsidiary, Abdullah Al Othaim Markets, has hundreds of branches across Saudi Arabia and Egypt, employing over 16,500 people. Al Othaim Real Estate Company owns multiple entertainment centers, fashion retail stores, and shopping malls in the GCC and North Africa.

The company's leadership transition played a crucial role in its success. Abdullah Al Othaim learned the principles of trade from his father and was able to build on that foundation. The company also adopted a multi-generational leadership approach, gradually allowing his sons to assume leadership roles after his death. The close relationship between generations enabled the new leaders to gain practical experience and guidance while preserving the company's values.

Shared values have been an integral part of the company's success. The vision has been passed down between generations, deepening their understanding of the company's foundations and purpose.

Al Othaim Holding Subsidiaries

The Othaim Markets Company

It is a public joint stock company working in wholesale and retail foodstuff trade and is classified among the largest 100 companies in the Middle East. It has a wide geographical spread throughout Saudi Arabia, with its presence in more than 100 regions and cities, and it owns many branches that have reached more than 342 within the Kingdom, in addition to its spread outside the Kingdom 46 branches in the Arab Republic of Egypt.

www.othaimmarkets.com

Abdullah Al Othaim Investment Company

It is one of the most prominent companies in the Saudi economic sector. Its main activity includes establishing, operating, and managing major commercial centers, hospitality, real estate investment, entertainment and cinema, artificial intelligence, fashion, restaurants and cafes, and others. It has 12 commercial complexes.
