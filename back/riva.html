RIVA Investment

RIVA Investment is the diversifier of Al Othaim Holding as the international investment hub while adhering to the Best-in-class Family Office Standards to develop and improve the efficiency of the investment and the sustainability of the returns.

At RIVA Investment, we work to develop investment mechanisms, diversify the investment portfolio, and develop the investment strategy by directing the family’s investments to many diverse sectors such as financial, real estate, insurance, health, education, lighting, technology, information technology, and other active industries in multiple countries. RIVA Investment targets long-term investments with significant companies in global markets and the trading market.

What Inspires US

We are focused on reasonable wealth creation that will enable current and future generations to unlock opportunities while caring for the future in an ever-changing world. Hard work, self-sufficiency, and discipline will be the foundation for our success.

RIVA Vision Pillars

01. Global Network
Embracing the value of a global network by fostering strategic partnerships and collaborations and leveraging diverse perspectives to drive innovation, expand market reach, and create value across borders.
02. Sustainable Annuity
Committing to sustainable annuity by focusing on long-term, steady growth and profitability while considering the environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors to ensure responsible and sustainable business practices.
03. Resilient Capital Growth
Striving for resilient capital growth by maintaining a solid financial foundation, managing risks effectively, and making strategic investments that deliver consistent and sustainable returns to stakeholders.
04. Diversified
Emphasizing the value of diversification by expanding into multiple markets, diversifying products, and building a resilient business model that can withstand market fluctuations and economic uncertainties.
05. Governance
Upholding strong governance practices by promoting transparency, accountability, and ethical behavior across all levels of the organization, ensuring compliance with regulations, protecting stakeholder interests, and maintaining high standards of corporate governance.
