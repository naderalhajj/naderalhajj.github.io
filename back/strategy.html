Wealth preservation and growth

Our strategy focuses on private investments that offer extensive exposure to global private markets, presenting promising long-term return potential. By allocating funds across diverse sub-asset classes and leveraging various investment styles, we aim to achieve superior risk-adjusted returns compared to conventional public market equities. This approach prioritizes the preservation and growth of wealth for high-net-worth individuals, enabling them to unlock opportunities and create a secure financial future.

Prudent risk management

We are deeply committed to proactively managing risks to safeguard our assets and the future of our stakeholders. As a prudent, long-term investor, we strive to identify and mitigate all risks to the best of our ability, aligning with industry standards and international best practices.

Value creation through strategic investment

We believe all investments have a distinct path for value creation, independent of their underlying market direction. We aim to maximize returns for our high-net-worth clients by leveraging our expertise and proactive value-creation initiatives. Our strategy focuses on identifying opportunities and implementing investment processes that generate value, ultimately enhancing our stakeholders' wealth and financial well-being.

Building a world-class organization

RIVA works towards long-term targets to build a strong foundation with broadly diversified exposure and top-tier managers. Through steady progress, we strive to create a world-class family office that delivers exceptional investment outcomes for our clients. This commitment to excellence permeates every aspect of our organization, ensuring we continuously improve and provide the highest level of service and expertise.

Long-term partnerships

We value continuity and the belief in nurturing new talents, as true partnerships are built over the long term. Our strategy emphasizes the importance of establishing enduring relationships based on trust and collaboration, aiming to create a world-class family office and unlock opportunities for the benefit of all stakeholders.

Company Governance

Strong corporate governance is an integral part of the Company's core values and affects how we direct our company and our relationship with interested parties. We believe that the highest standards of corporate governance are essential to our business integrity, performance, and sustainable growth mission. RIVA is committed to having sound corporate governance principles and practices to ensure compliance and alignment with the relevant regulations and best practices.
